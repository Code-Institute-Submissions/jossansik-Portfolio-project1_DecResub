Welcome to Krusboda

Built in 1970-75

Located in Tyresö municipality in the southern Stockholm county, Krusboda is a residential area which comprises approximately 1500 homes in varied low-rise buildings in functionalist style architecture.

Surrounded by nature

In direct connection to Krusboda is both Tyresta National Park and Alby nature reserve with beautiful and valuable natural environment and great opportunities for outdoor life and recreation.

A great community

In the car free, child-friendly area there is a school and three preschools as well as a grocery store and pizza restaurant. The residents of Krusboda also enjoy a communal pool facility with sauna.
