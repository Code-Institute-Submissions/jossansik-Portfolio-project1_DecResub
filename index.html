What is Krusboda?

Built in 1970-75

Located in Tyresö municipality in Stockholm county, Krusboda is a residential area which comprises approximately 1500 homes in varied low-rise buildings in functionalist style architecture.

Surrounded by nature

In direct connection to Krusboda is Tyresta National Park with the largest area of ancient forest in the southern part of Sweden. There is also Alby nature reserve where you'll find beautiful and valuable natural environment with great opportunities for outdoor life, recreation and education..

A great community

In the area there is a school, three preschools and a square with grocery store and pizza restaurant. The area also has a communal pool facility with sauna.
